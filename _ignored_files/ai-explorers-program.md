AI Explorers Program
Background
In CMS, AI is an emerging technology with various level across components. To create opportunities to explore AI and decrease the barriers to entry and pain points for AI projects, we are launching this AI Explorers program in CMS, open to all federal employees. This program will provide opportunities for Federal employees to learn about AI and test AI hypotheses through the funding and mentorship of small AI related pilots.
Program Highlights
·       Encourage interested federal employees to submit proposals for AI hypotheses they want to explore through a pilot.
·       A vetting process selects submissions for execution
·       Funding is made available by OIT for up to $250K per pilot
·       6-8 Pilots per year. Open submission quarterly throughout the FY2022
·       Components manage their own pilots with OIT mentorship and assistance.
·       Knowledge gleaned is shared across CMS
Key points
·       Starting time: FY2022
·       Funding limit: up to $250,000 for each project
·       Time span: 3-6 months for each funding project
Submission
·       Submit your idea here: https://cms.gov1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_8jqSX0YFO2SU5Yq
·       Deadline for submission*: Feb 18th, 2022
*accepting submission on a quarterly basis.
What to expect after submission
·       First screen results within 3 weeks after close of submission
·       Participant past the first screen will have opportunity for a short presentation
·       Final award will be announced within 8 weeks after close of submission
