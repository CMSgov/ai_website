CMS AI Playbook

Does AI sound as overwhelming as it does promising? Don’t worry. The CMS AI Playbook is an easy-to-follow guide that walks stakeholders through the process of conceiving, developing, and implementing AI solutions at CMS. It covers design principles, research and development, engineering, and procurement.

The guidelines in the AI Playbook cover the technical aspects of development as well as human factors. They are not just for software engineers. They are invaluable to anybody at CMS who believes an AI solution can improve their team or provide value to their customer. Successful application of AI must take into account the customer perspective; it requires well-tailored, high quality data sources; it must be secure; and last but not least, it must follow rigorous ethical standards.

To learn more about how to responsibly, effectively, and efficiently devise AI solutions, check out the official CMS AI Playbook
