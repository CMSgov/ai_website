CMS AI Explorers Program

The AI Explorers Program is an exciting opportunity for CMS employees to prototype AI applications for testing and potential development. Each quarter for two years, OIT will accept proposals for projects that apply AI and ML techniques related to CMS's mission and to the applicant's team functional area. From these submissions, OIT executive leadership will fund two projects per quarter up to $250,000 each. Along with funding, awardees will receive support such as matching with pre-qualified vendors. They will have three to six months to test their hypotheses.

The goals of the pilot are to:

  • bootstrap both proofs of value and initial Minimum Viable Products
  • encourage applicants to get acquainted with AI/ML and grow their technological skill sets
  • test out hypotheses using Agile methodologies prior to a large-scale development effort
  • develop incremental expertise, and boost confidence in the AI domain
  • establish a baseline framework on how to bring an AI/ML project from ideation to production readiness within CMS

Background

The private sector has adopted AI technologies much more rapidly than government agencies. To accelerate AI research and applications throughout the federal government, Congress passed The National AI Initiative Act of 2020 (DIVISION E, SEC. 5001), which became law on January 1, 2021. The National AI Initiative provides a coordinated program across the entire Federal government to accelerate AI research and develop applications that will increase the Nation’s economic prosperity and national security.

AI Explorers is a major effort by OIT to comply with the NAIIA, reducing barriers to entry for AI projects at CMS. By increasing the application of AI across components, we can increase value to our customers, to healthcare providers, to Medicaid and Medicare enrollees, and to the American public

Deadlines

The program accepts project idea submissions on a quarterly basis throughout FY2022.

If you've applied in previous rounds, your application is still active for re-consideration and a resubmission is not needed

Eligibility

Any federal CMS employee is eligible to apply.
